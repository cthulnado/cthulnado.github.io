[ PROJECTION NOTES ]

On Watching Films in This Place

The Lantern Reel is less concerned with verdicts than with impressions. A film may be written about because it is great, because it is strange, because it is beloved, or because something in it refused to let go.

Expect old favorites, first viewings, rewatches, and the occasional entry for something imperfect but memorable.
