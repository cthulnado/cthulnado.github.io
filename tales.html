On the Nature of These Tales

This corner of The Undercroft is reserved for fiction and near-fiction: old ideas, new stories, fragments rescued from notebooks, and pieces that may one day become something larger.

Some stories may share settings, objects, rumors, or names. Some may stand entirely alone. The shelves do not insist on order, only atmosphere.
